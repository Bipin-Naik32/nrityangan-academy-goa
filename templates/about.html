About Us

Preserving Tradition • Inspiring Excellence

Nrityangan Academy Goa

Nrityangan Academy Goa is a premier institution dedicated to preserving, promoting, and propagating the rich heritage of Indian classical dance, with a special focus on Bharatanatyam.

Rooted in the timeless values of the Guru–Shishya Parampara, the academy strives to nurture not only skilled performers but also disciplined, confident, and culturally aware individuals.

At Nrityangan, dance is more than an art form—it is a journey of self-discovery, discipline, and artistic expression.

Our Vision

To preserve and promote the rich heritage of Indian classical dance while nurturing artists who embody excellence, discipline, cultural values, and artistic integrity.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality training in Bharatanatyam.
  • Develop confidence and creativity.
  • Encourage artistic expression.
  • Create opportunities for performances and competitions.
  • Preserve Indian cultural heritage.

Examinations & Certification

Students are encouraged to appear for graded Bharatanatyam examinations conducted by Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mandal (ABGMVM), helping them develop practical skills, theoretical knowledge, stage confidence, and academic credentials.

"At Nrityangan Academy Goa, we believe in blending artistic excellence with structured learning, enabling every student to grow as a confident performer and a lifelong ambassador of India's cultural heritage."
